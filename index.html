Instructions

1. Press space to shoot the zombies.

2. There are total 200 bullets for shooting the zombies.

3. Increase the score to 180 for winning the game.

4. There are three lives. If the zombie touches the player 1 life will be reduced. If all the 3 lives are used game will be ended.

5. If the zombies eats all the brains. The game will be ended.

6. If you want to move the zombie upwards press the up arrow key and to move it downwards press the down arrow key but you can't move the zombie rightwards and leftwards.

7. If you want to destroy all the zombies together then press B but you will have 10 bombs only so use them wisely.

8. If the game is ended then press space or the restart icon to restart the game.

9. Remember, game will be ended in 3 conditions, if your all the lives are used up or all the brains are eaten up or your bullets are finished.

10. In order to win the game use your bombs and bullets wisely, and make the score 180.

11. Click on the icon given below to play the game.
